# AI Interviewer --- Overview

## 1. Interview Start

During account setup, the user provides their name. The AI interviewer
uses this name during sessions to create a more realistic and personal
interview experience.

The interview begins with a professional greeting to simulate a real
interview environment. The AI welcomes the interviewee by name, briefly
explains the structure of the session, and transitions directly into the
first question.

Example behavior: - Professional tone - Personalized greeting using the
user's name - Clear expectations - Immediate transition into the first
question

The purpose of this design is to maintain realism while minimizing
unnecessary friction or awkward setup.

## 6. Core Product Philosophy

This AI Interviewer is designed to: - Simulate realistic interview
pressure - Reduce anxiety through safe repetition - Encourage structured
thinking - Provide measurable improvement over time - Maintain
professional tone while remaining supportive

The AI acts as a realistic interviewer first, and a coach second.

## 19. Session Length (v1)

The interview session consists of two primary questions. This length is
intentionally short to allow rapid practice iterations and faster
feedback cycles while still exercising the full interview flow. Future
versions may increase the number of questions once stability and realism
are validated.
